Explore the Planets

Mercury

Smallest planet, closest to the Sun, and has the most extreme temperature variations.

Venus

Thick atmosphere causes a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet.

Earth

The only planet known to support life with diverse ecosystems and water coverage.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet due to iron oxide, and has the tallest volcano, Olympus Mons.

Jupiter

The largest planet with a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Famous for its beautiful ring system made of ice and rock.

Uranus

Rotates on its side and has extreme seasonal changes.

Neptune

The farthest planet, known for its strong winds and deep blue color.
